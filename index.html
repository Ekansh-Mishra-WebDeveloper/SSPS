About Us

Sir Syed Public School

Founded in 1992, Sir Syed Public School stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Unnao, Uttar Pradesh. Affiliated with the ICSE board, our school has consistently nurtured thousands of students into accomplished individuals who contribute meaningfully to society.

Located at Priyadarshini Nagar, Near Achal Ganj Bypass, Unnao, we provide a stimulating environment that blends academic rigour with moral values, preparing students for the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To be the foremost institution fostering intellectual, ethical, and holistic development.

Our Mission

Delivering quality education aligned with ICSE standards to shape future leaders.

Our Values

Integrity, compassion, excellence, and respect for diversity in all endeavours.

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-wishers,

At Sir Syed Public School, we believe that true education transcends mere academic achievement. Our institution, since its establishment in 1992, has been dedicated to nurturing well-rounded individuals equipped with knowledge, character, and the resilience to face the world with confidence.

Guided by our cherished motto — "O my lord Increase me in knowledge" — we strive to instil a lifelong love for learning in every student. With a dedicated team of 57 teachers and a vibrant community of 10,000+ students, we remain committed to maintaining our legacy of 96% board results and comprehensive development.

— Sadia Kamal, Principal
